About the presenter:

Nick Cavalancia, Microsoft Cloud and Datacenter MVP

Nick has more than 25 years of enterprise IT experience and is a Microsoft Cloud and Data Center MVP with a focus on cybersecurity. He is an accomplished consultant, speaker, trainer, writer, and columnist. He has earned MCSE, MCT, Master CNE, and Master CNI certifications. He has authored, co-authored, and contributed to nearly two dozen books and regularly speaks, writes, and blogs for some of the most recognized tech companies today on a variety of topics including cyber security, business continuity, cloud adoption, and compliance in markets including healthcare, financial services, and Retail.
